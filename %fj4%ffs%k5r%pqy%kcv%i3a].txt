===== 一些观点 =====

艺术属于每一个人，艺术的创作应是开放、自由、平等的。 \\ Art belongs to everyone; \\ Art making should be open, free, and equal to everyone. \\  \\ 艺术不应只是奢侈品、收藏品或当代都市时尚的一个文化消费品门类。享受艺术和参加文化生活是联合国《世界人权宣言》第二十七条所规定的一项人人享有的基本权利。 \\ Art should not be mere luxury items, collectibles, or cultural consumer products in today's urban fashion. Rather, according to Article 27 of Universal Declaration of Human Rights issued by the United Nations, to enjoy the arts is a basic human right for everyone.

----

艺术创作者是最深入艺术的人，也是从艺术中获益最深的人。 \\ Those who make art are the ones who go the deepest into it and the ones who get the most out of it. \\  \\ 观赏者、鉴赏者、消费者、收藏者的艺术体验和与艺术的关系从根本上不同于艺术创作者。只有艺术创作给每个人重新发明艺术和重新发明自己的机会。 \\ The experience of spectators, connoisseurs, consumers, and collectors have a fundamentally different relationship with art and a fundamentally different experience of art from those who make art. Only art making gives everyone a chance to reinvent art and to reinvent oneself.

----

当代艺术只有在创作过程中才最真实地存在。 \\ Contemporary art exists only in the process of becoming. \\  \\ 如果说当代艺术是正在进行的不间断的实验性创造，那么从严格意义上讲，唯一真正当代的艺术是正在被创作的艺术；其余的都是传统、经典、遗骸。艺术不是名词，而是动词。一件当代艺术作品并不是一个空间里放着的一个东西，而是你做的一个东西或事情所正在打开的一个空间。 \\ If we think of contemporary art as ongoing and endless experimental creation, then strictly speaking, the only truly contemporary art is the one that is in the process of creation; all else is tradition, classics, relics - for art is not a noun, but a verb. A work of contemporary art is not a thing that you see in a space, it is a space being created by a thing you make or do.
